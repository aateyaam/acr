Alumni Relations

The Alumni Relations team is an intermediary between IIT Hyderabad and its alumni. The team’s key responsibility is to encourage alumni to remain connected and give back to their alma mater. A dedicated team actively works towards involving our alumni in several important initiatives at the Institute.

Corporate Relations

The Corporate Relations team supports the institute's research and education mission by attracting, engaging, and cultivating a broad spectrum of strategic partnerships with corporates & connects with them to understand their social impact areas and encourages to collaborate with IITH to take their CSR initiative forward.

Public Relations

The Public relations team plays an instrumental role in building IIT Hyderabad’s branding. The team’s key responsibility includes leveraging various forms of communication (social media, press releases, etc.) to keep alumni as well as the general public updated about the Institute’s milestones and its important initiatives and activities.

Career Services

The Office of Career Services hopes to work on a defined structure of hiring procedure and will facilitate the interactions with various industrial, management and research organisations ensuring the students are given enough exposure/industrial training right from the second year enabling them to be employed by the best of the firms.
